About

Dr. Kyra Rudy is an Assistant Professor of Mechanical Engineering in the Caterpillar College of Engineering & Technology at Bradley University. Dr. Rudy received her PhD in Mechanical Engineering from Northwestern University and a Bachelor’s in Mechanical Engineering from Cleveland State University. Previously, she served as an Instructor of Engineering at Loyola University Chicago. In addition to teaching real human students, Dr. Rudy also works as an AI Consultant, teaching large language models (LLMs) how to tackle complex engineering problems. Dr. Rudy’s research interests are rooted in robotic applications in rehabilitation engineering, with an emphasis on developing more informative models of human ability to facilitate personalized and targeted assistance.
